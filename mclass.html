Helmer and Miguel's Really Cool Project


Your friend was acting suspicious and somebody alerted the guards. You hear over the intercome that a lockdown is taking place. The security guards spend 2 hours serching for your friend. You had to hold in your urges to go to the bathroom and now you must wait throuht traffic until you can relive yourself.


This game takes you through the rigorous day-to-day occurences of the UCVTS school district. From culinary fire drills in UCTECH to 2-hour-long lockdowns, this game is sure to knock your socks off and give you the full unfiltered UCVTS experience!
