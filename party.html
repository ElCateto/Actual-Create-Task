Helmer and Miguel's Really Cool Project


You are a famous person so you choose to party till you dropo dead. You party so much in shady Japanese nightclubs that you come across a tall, muscular yakuza gang member who wants your money. You refuse to hand over the two dollars you have in your pocket. An action movie fight scene plays out and he pushes you off the roof of the nightclub to your death. Sayonara Sucker!


This game takes you through the rigorous day-to-day occurences of the UCVTS school district. From culinary fire drills in UCTECH to 2-hour-long lockdowns, this game is sure to knock your socks off and give you the full unfiltered UCVTS experience!
